About

Welcoming you warmly. My name is Emre Gözübüyük and I am a professional breathwork practitioner and flow artist based in Germany. I completed a two-year breathwork training with Alchemy of Breath and have been offering sessions online and in person for over three years.

Alongside my breathwork practice, I passionately explore flow arts with LED and fire props. Between ember and breath lies a deep connection to the dance of life, and this is where my inspiration resides for all that I offer.
