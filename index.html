About

Welcoming you warmly. My name is Emre Gözübüyük and I am a professional breathwork practitioner and flow artist based in Germany. I have done my 2 years of breathwork training with the Alchemy of Breath and have been offering sessions online and in presence over 3 years. Next to my breath career I passionately pursue expressing flow arts through led and fire toys. Between ember and breath lies a deep connection to the dance of life and this is where my inspiration resides for all that I offer.
